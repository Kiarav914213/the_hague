Privacy Policy

thehague-taxi.nl, part of Hano Enterprise VOF, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

What information do we collect?

  1. Personal identification data:
    • Name
    • Address
    • Phone number
    • Email
  2. Payment information:
    • Bank account number
    • Credit card details
  3. Trip information:
    • Pick-up/drop-off location
    • Time & date
    • Preferences, special requests
  4. Technical data:
    • IP address
    • Browser type
    • Device info

How do we use your data?

  • To provide taxi service
  • Manage bookings & payments
  • Send confirmations & updates
  • Improve user experience

How do we protect your data?

We use strong technical and organizational security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Do we share your data?

We only share with third parties when necessary for:

  • Processing payments
  • Maintaining and improving services
  • Legal compliance

Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Correct or delete data
  • Request processing restriction
  • Withdraw consent
  • Transfer your data

Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience and analyze traffic. Learn more in our cookie policy.
