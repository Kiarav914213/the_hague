Group transport

Group transport

Here at The Hague Taxi we provide group transportation for every occasion. Ranging from short transfers for small groups to large corporate events for dozens of passengers, we offer a safe, reliable service for our wide range of clients at affordable prices. At The Hague Taxi you are assured of a pleasant and safe ride. Our drivers put you in the center of attention and are happy to accommodate your requests. Do you want the radio in the taxi bus to a certain station? No problem! Do you want the air conditioning to blow a little louder? That is also no problem. All of The Hague Taxi’s taxi buses are incredibly luxurious and above all look neat.

Partly because of the appearance of our taxi vans, you can also use them for business transport. For example, do you need to go to a certain location in The Hague with a few colleagues? We focus on both private and business transport.

If you would like to book group transport with The Hague Taxi, we are happy to assist you on +31 70 783 1111. You can also use our Whatsapp service. Easily arrange a taxi via Whatsapp!
